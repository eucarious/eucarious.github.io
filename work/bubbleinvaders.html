BUBBLE INVADERS

Unity   Solo   7 weeks itch.io page

SUMMARY

Bubble Invaders is a Space Invaders copy with an underwater theme made for the very first game project course at Metropolia.

While not the most impressive work, it allowed me to experience what goes into finishing a game project from start to end.

DETAILS

All of Bubble Invaders' sprites were made by me in Clip Studio Paint and Aseprite. Sprites and animations for the enemies, the player, and shelters were made in Aseprite and the background was made in CSP.

The game features a slight parallax effect, which can be seen in the play mode.

While the game still has many issues, I've decided to leave it as is, as it's what I managed to deliver in the given time with my skills at the time.
