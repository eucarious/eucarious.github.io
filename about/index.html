about me

Hi there!

I'm Viktor, a game development student at Metropolia.

If you couldn't tell by now, I like video games! I'm particularly interested by the pixels on the screen and making things look nice.

On this website, you can find what I've been able to make while combining characters into code and hoping it compiles. These projects may range from university projects to personal adventures to professional work.

Outside of staring at code, I also stare at canvases, words, moving images— just a whole bunch of things— and, of course, games. You'll likely find traces of whatever I've been interested in during that time in my personal projects. Many of my adventures into other fields can be found online.

That's be all from me!

See you around!
— Viktor
